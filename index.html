About Me

Hello! I am Raghurai!

Born in the city of knowledge or oxford of the east Pune, INDIA, I have a passion for creating interactive experiences with emerging technologies I am a 3rd semester graduate student in the Human-Centered Computing program at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.

I visualize and design experiences at the intersection of human requirements and technology availability. I was introduced to design from various experience but the main influence I received while I was working in a company in India, UX Design for me is helping users achieve their end goals without being stressed. I believe in Weiser's vision for the future where computing contextually serves humans. Apart from Users, wireframes and pixels, A sport of competitive swimming take a good amount of my time, I have been a completive swimmer for around 14 years and also serving as a swimming coach for UMBC, watching swimmers execute the strokes with such art and grace is a rewarding spectacle. While it can be a very competitive and visually beautiful performance, the spectators don’t always realize that the coaches help swimmers study every aspect of their stroke. Similarly, designers undergo lengthy technical planning in order to create a smooth and effective user interface for their customers. Swimming and UX both require understanding how humans will perceive a visual or sometimes auditory presentation.

Why You Should Fill Your Company with Athletes?

I believe that athletes have a wonderful advantage to succeed in the professional world too, so long as they treat business/industry like sport ,follow their passions, keep an open mind to new opportunities, and refuse to give up.
