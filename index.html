About Me

Hello! I am Raghurai!

Born in the city of knowledge or oxford of the east Pune, INDIA, I have a passion for creating interactive experiences with emerging technologies I am a 3rd semester graduate student in the Human-Centered Computing program at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.

I visualize and design experiences at the intersection of human requirements and technology availability. I was introduced to design from various experience but the main influence I received while I was working in a company in India, UX Design for me is helping users achieve their end goals without being stressed. I believe in Weiser's vision for the future where computing contextually serves humans. Apart from Users, wireframes and pixels, A sport of competitive swimming take a good amount of my time, I have been a completive swimmer for around 14 years and also serving as a swimming coach for UMBC, watching swimmers execute the strokes with such art and grace is a rewarding spectacle.
